## Product (high level)

- B2B tool to complete security and compliance questionnaires using suggestions derived from customer-uploaded documents (policies, SOC 2 reports, etc.) and configured context.
- Private RAG-style architecture: policies stored in an encrypted, tenant-isolated vector database; customer documents are not used to train third-party foundation models.
- Human review before export or external sharing; confidence scores and review workflows are part of the product positioning.
- **SOC 2 Type II:** in progress (not stated as completed on the public site).
- **Data location:** Marketing states EU data centers (e.g. Frankfurt, Germany) and GDPR-first processing; details in Privacy Policy and DPA.
- **Questionnaires:** Marketing copy uses examples such as “200+ row” Excel files; actual row counts depend on customer files.

### File formats (from FAQ)

- **Questionnaire upload:** .xlsx, .csv, .docx
- **Policy documents:** PDF, .docx, .txt, plain text
- **Exports:** Delivered in the original questionnaire format (e.g. structure-preserving .xlsx).

### Accuracy (from FAQ)

- Internal testing reference: 91–97% answer accuracy across 1,200+ questionnaire rows when the knowledge base contains relevant documentation; answers below 85% confidence flagged for review.

## Pricing (from public pricing section)

Prices and entitlements below match the live marketing configuration.

### Free

- **Price:** $0 / month
- **Description:** For founders exploring the product
- **Includes:** 1 questionnaire per month; up to 5 policy documents; basic Trust Center page; Excel & CSV export

### Starter

- **Price:** $299 / month (monthly) or $239 / month (annual billing)
- **Description:** For Series A teams closing enterprise deals
- **Includes:** 10 questionnaires per month; up to 50 policy documents; branded Trust Center; Excel, CSV & Word export; email support

### Pro

- **Price:** $799 / month (monthly) or $639 / month (annual billing)
- **Description:** For scaling teams with a dedicated sales motion
- **Includes:** Unlimited questionnaires; unlimited policy documents; advanced Trust Center (NDA-gated docs); 5 team seats; EU AI Act evidence pack; priority AI processing; Slack & HubSpot integration; priority support

### Enterprise

- **Price:** Custom
- **Description:** For companies with strict compliance requirements
- **Includes:** Everything in Pro; SSO / SAML 2.0; BYOK (Bring Your Own LLM Key); Salesforce & HubSpot CRM sync; dedicated account manager; SLA guarantees; on-premise RAG option; custom data residency (EU)
